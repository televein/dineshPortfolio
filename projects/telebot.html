Tele Chatbot

Tele Chatbot is an educational web chatbot tailored for students in 10th, 11th, and 12th grades. It offers a repository of previous year questions, study materials, and textbooks. Developed exclusively with React.js, Tele Chatbot aims to streamline studying processes and enhance educational experiences for students.
